The data in this visualisation reflects the daily weather information for capital cities around the world from 29 August 2023 to 08 September 2023.

Continental Wind Speed Variations

Pacific: High Wind Speed (North)

During late summer months, high occurrence of tropical cyclones and typhoons bring strong winds from North and East across Pacific Ocean.

Atlantic: High Wind Speed (South East)

High wind speeds in the southeast direction in the Atlantic Ocean can be influenced by the movement of tropical cyclones (hurricanes or typhoons).

Forest Fires

In regions such as Indonesia, Chile, and parts of China , the burning of forests contribute to air pollution, especially during dry seasons.

Industrialization

Rapid industrialization in countries like China and parts of Indonesia increases emissions of carbon monoxide from factories and vehicles.

Biomass Burning

In agricultural regions like Indonesia, the biomass burning for land clearing releases carbon monoxide into the air.

Unveiling the Ozone Hotspots

Ozone is a vital component of the Earth's atmosphere. It shields us from harmful solar radiation, creating the ozone layer. However, when it's found at ground level, it becomes a major air pollutant.

These top 20 countries, each with its own unique story, showcase the urgent need for action to mitigate ozone pollution and safeguard the air we breathe.
